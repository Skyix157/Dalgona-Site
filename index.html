Tutorial

Give each cookie an NFC sticker. When a guest taps the sticker, this page opens. They can watch the tutorial first, then upload their dalgona photo and name.

  • 1
    Pick up your dalgona
    Check the shape on the cookie.
  • 2
    Use the needle carefully
    Trace around the shape slowly.
  • 3
    Do not crack it
    Try to remove the shape cleanly.
  • 4
    Take a photo
    Upload your result to the gallery.
